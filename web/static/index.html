Loopie is here to help you stay on top of your tasks and meetings, it can...
  • Schedule and update calendar events (and invite attendees)
  • Create, list, and complete tasks
  • Meeting prep and follow-ups (including transcript-based recaps when available)
  • Save/search notes and link recaps to a meeting
